# A Moment in Time

The sun dips low behind the hills,
The evening air grows soft and still.
A single bird calls out its song—
The day has been, the night is long.

We walk through fields of fading light,
Where shadows stretch and merge with night.
The world slows down, the mind grows clear,
And all that matters feels quite near.

So take this moment, hold it close,
For time moves on—we know it goes.
But here, right now, we have this breath:
A small eternity before the rest.
